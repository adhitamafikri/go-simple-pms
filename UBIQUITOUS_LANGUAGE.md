## Ubiquitous Language

1. User

It's anyone who are using our platforms, be it our web platform or the control panel dashboard

2. Role

The identifier that is tied to the users registered in our system and controls how the system should behave based on the roles attached to the user who is trying to access our resources

3. Property

The land or building or even a sole room that is acquired and managed by our company. This could be a villa, apartment, or apart-hotel

4. Listing

A sellable unit of the property. We can say this simply as a room from villas, apartments, and apart-hotels that can be reserved within a certain range of date for our guests to stay

5. Reservation

Can be simply said as an act of locking a resource (_in this case the listing_) as a commitment for our guest to make sure that the reserved resource remains available until the guest successfully paid for it

7. Stay

Once our guest successfully paid their reservation, our system would create a resource with the term of 'stay', which can be put simply as a reserved resource that is paid by the guest and can be used/consumed by the guest
